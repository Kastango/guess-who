Guess Who?

Game rules:

Begin by picking from the descriptions at the top. Example with the Aliens, click the "Hat?" to see if they are wearing a hat. If we say, “yes,” you flip over all of the characters without a hat. If we say, “no,” you flip over the characters that have a hat. Through the process of elimination, you will eventually be able to “guess” the name of the character. Keep in mind these are yes or no questions and you only have a set amount of guesses. If your guesses run out before you enter in the correct name, you lose. If you enter the wrong name you will lose a guess. If you enter the right name before your guesses run out you win! See the leader board to compare your results against other players!
